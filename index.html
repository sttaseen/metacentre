About Us

MetaCentre NZ Limited is an emerging company comprising a group of experienced naval architects and marine engineers, marine surveyors, cargo inspectors, and other technical and project management professionals.

Our staffs are highly qualified specialists with marine, aeronautical and business background. We have both sea-going and shore-based expertise to sustain our services at the highest possible standard. In order to provide a broad range of services to our clients, we maintain a close network with other professional service providers including licensed ship, yacht and boat builders, marine accident investigators, computer modeling specialists, and other facilities that cater for all common needs of the maritime industry.
